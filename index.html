E10s training game, by Alice Heuz on Phoenix

You can use keyboard arrows, mouse or touchscreen to move your character around.
Difficulty :
Easy : Display cleaved half
Normal : Normal mechanic setting
Hard : Only a few seconds to move, same timing as the Voidgates then Giga Slash mechanic
Very hard : No side callout, even less time than in the fight
Drunk : After trying to forget you wiped in Sastasha
Rotate the camera to be behind the shadow (easy and normal difficulties only)
